WHO WE ARE

About Aurora
Energy Services

Aurora Energy specializes in delivering OIMR and asset integrity maintenance services. Our experienced team is dedicated to ensuring the highest standards of quality and safety in all our operations — from offshore project planning and execution to subsea inspections and port management.

Headquartered in Ghana and operating across Nigeria, Equatorial Guinea, Angola, and the United Kingdom, we bring certified expertise and a safety-first culture to every engagement across the Gulf of Guinea Basin.

WHAT WE DO

Our Core Services

From offshore project planning to port operations, Aurora Energy delivers end-to-end marine services with certified expertise and an unwavering commitment to safety at every stage.

01
⚙
ENGINEERING & PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Onshore and offshore engineering covering every phase of the project lifecycle — planning, execution, installation, completions, commissioning, startup, plug & abandonment, and skilled manpower.

02
🌊
MARINE & SUBSEA SOLUTIONS

Advanced ROV services for precise underwater inspections and interventions. Air and saturation diving expertise for complex underwater tasks maintaining subsea infrastructure integrity.

03
🔍
ASSETS INTEGRITY & RISK MANAGEMENT

Structure repair, CP/CD surveys, leak investigation and remediation, platform and pipeline inspection, FPSO hull and turret inspections, and flooded member detection surveys.

04
🏗
SUBSEA CONSTRUCTION & MAINTENANCE

Full range of subsea construction services delivered by proficiency-certified personnel: structure repair, component change-out, mid-water arch inspection, FMD surveys, and anchor chain inspection.

05
⚓
PORT MANAGEMENT SERVICES

Vessel Traffic Services (VTS), piloting with Tugs and AHTS vessels, buoy and navigational system installation and maintenance, bathymetric surveys, dredging, and marine safety solutions — ensuring safe and efficient maritime operations.
